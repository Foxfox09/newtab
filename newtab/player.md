# üéµ Music Player

**YouTube Music** integration has been added, allowing you to control music playback directly from the new tab.

### Features
- Displays cover art, track title, and artist.
- Playback controls: play/pause, next/previous track.
- "Like" and "dislike" buttons.
- Smooth track progress bar.

### Commands
- `//player on` ‚Äî enable the player.
- `//player off` ‚Äî disable the player.
- `//play <song name>` ‚Äî search and play music. If no name is provided, it will just open YouTube Music.

### Important
1.  **Open Tab Required**: For the player to work, you must have a tab with [music.youtube.com](https://music.youtube.com) open in your browser.
2.  **Styling**: The player's appearance automatically adapts to the style you have selected for the page (`//style 1` or `//style 2`).
